We are going Carbon Negative

and on our way to B Corp

Our world needs us

You’ve seen the impact that plastic has on our oceans and heard about how carbon emissions can contribute to global warming… Well, here at RankHive, we really care about our planet and we want to do what we can to help protect it and also try to educate others about environmental causes.

In addition to our pro-bono programme, we are regularly involved in projects that help the environment and promote sustainability. Whether we are donating our time or our money, it’s always for a good cause.

Sustainable web design

We practice sustainable web design, which means we make our websites as lightweight and as low-carbon as possible. In 2022, we helped save 148 trees from websites alone! In additional to reducing the amount of carbon emitted from the websites by over 5 tonnes, we have improved the performance, helping each business land more sales too.

As proud members of BIMA, it was a no-brainer to sign up for their Net Carbon Negative programme back in 2020. This course changed everything in our company as it opened our eyes to the impact we were having on the environment.

Since then, our director has joined the Sustainability Council, actively helping drive change within the digital sector and educating others on how to create sustainable campaigns and build low-carbon websites.

Go carbon negative

We’re so happy we found this amazing non-profit organisation! Ecologi helps to compensate your carbon footprint and fund climate projects. They are fully transparent and show proof of trees being planted, carbon being offset and even share their financials. We are currently helping projects in Guatemala and Uganda and will continue to contribute to more throughout the year. We’d love to ask you to help us contribute by visiting our page and planting us a tree!

Plant us a tree

We are consistently trying to support charities and organisations who do good in the world, but it’s not easy to know who to donate to. That’s why we’ve now signed up to One Percent for the Planet and will donate 1% of our gross sales every year to their non-profit partners. By joining this incredible network, we are now connected with other fantastic companies who also like giving back. Furthermore, we have more opportunities to find out which non-profits could do with our creative services that we will be able to offer pro-bono.
